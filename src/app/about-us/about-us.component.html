About OMNI Hospitals

Omni Hospitals is a multi-specialty healthcare provider aimed at delivering the best patient care and innovative medical solutions. Omni Hospitals is a unit of Incor Group. Omni began its journey in 2010 at Kothapet. Over the last 15 years, it expanded its wings into 6 branches across 2 states, Telangana & Andhra Pradesh. In Hyderabad, we have branches in Kothapet, Kukatpally, and Nampally. In Andhra Pradesh, Kurnool and Vizag. It also offers Women & Childcare services through Giggles, which is located in Kukatpally & Vizag. Omni Hospitals is a leading multi-super specialty healthcare provider with world-class medical services across a wide range of specialties. Omni Hospitals has a team of 500 doctors and more than 1,000-bed capacity with 2000+ employees to provide exceptional care and support to patients.
